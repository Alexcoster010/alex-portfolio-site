§ 01  /  About

About Me

With experience spanning motorsports engineering, robotic automation, and manufacturing research, I thrive on projects that demand creativity, analysis, and practical execution. I have led the design of Formula SAE chassis and suspension systems, built robotic cells for CNC automation, and developed hardware spanning additive manufacturing and humanoid robotics.

My objective is to create solutions that push performance boundaries while remaining manufacturable and reliable — and, increasingly, to build the software and data tools that make those engineering decisions faster and better informed.
